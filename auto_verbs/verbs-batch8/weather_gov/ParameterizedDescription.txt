Get weather forecast from the National Weather Service.

Parameters:
- latitude: str — The latitude coordinate (default: "41.8781")
- longitude: str — The longitude coordinate (default: "-87.6298")
- max_results: int — Maximum number of forecast periods to return (default: 7)

Returns:
- A list of forecast periods, each with: period, temperature, wind_speed, wind_direction, forecast_summary, icon_description

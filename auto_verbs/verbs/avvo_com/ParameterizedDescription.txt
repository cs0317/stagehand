Avvo.com – Lawyer Search

Parameters:
  - specialty (str): Legal specialty to search for (e.g. "immigration")
  - location (str): City and state (e.g. "Los Angeles, CA")
  - max_results (int): Maximum number of lawyers to return (e.g. 5)

Steps:
  1. Navigate to https://www.avvo.com
  2. Search for {specialty} lawyers in {location}
  3. Extract up to {max_results} lawyers, each with:
     - name
     - rating
     - years of experience
     - number of reviews
  4. Return/print the list of lawyers

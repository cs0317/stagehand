Last.fm – Artist Search

Website: https://www.last.fm
Task: Search for an artist and extract profile info with top tracks

Parameters:
  - artist_name: artist to search for (e.g., "Radiohead")

Fields to extract:
  - artist_name: name of the artist
  - listeners: number of listeners
  - play_count: total play count
  - biography: biography summary
  - top_tracks: list of top 5 track names

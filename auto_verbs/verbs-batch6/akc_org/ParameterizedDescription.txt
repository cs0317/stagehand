AKC – Dog Breed Info

Look up a dog breed on akc.org and extract breed details.

Parameters:
  - breed (str): The breed name to search for (e.g., "Golden Retriever")

Returns:
  - breed_name (str): Official breed name
  - breed_group (str): Breed group (e.g., "Sporting")
  - size (str): Size category
  - life_expectancy (str): Life expectancy range
  - temperament (str): Temperament traits
  - popularity_rank (str): AKC popularity ranking

Open Library – Search Books by Author

Website: https://openlibrary.org
Task: Search for books by a given author

Parameters:
  - author: author name (e.g., "Isaac Asimov")

Fields to extract:
  - title: book title
  - author: book author
  - first_publish_year: year first published
  - num_editions: number of editions
  - cover_image_url: URL of cover image

Goodreads – Author Books

Search for an author on goodreads.com.
Navigate to their books page.
Compose a list of up to N top rated books.
Each book has its title, average rating, and number of ratings.
Print the list.

Parameters:
  author        – author name to search for (e.g. "Isaac Asimov")
  max_results   – maximum number of books to return (e.g. 5)

Look up an artist on Last.fm and extract profile info and top tracks.

Parameters:
- artist: Artist name (e.g. "Radiohead")
- max_tracks: Number of top tracks to extract (default 5)

Output:
- artist_name: Artist name
- listeners: Total listener count
- scrobbles: Total scrobble count
- bio: Short biography snippet
- top_tracks: List of {name, listeners} for top tracks

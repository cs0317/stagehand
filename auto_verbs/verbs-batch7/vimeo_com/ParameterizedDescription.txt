Vimeo – Video Search

Search Vimeo for videos by keyword.

Parameters:
- search_query (str): The search query (e.g. "short film documentary"). Default: "short film documentary"
- max_results (int): Maximum number of results to return. Default: 5

Returns:
- videos: list of VideoResult
  - title (str): Video title
  - creator (str): Creator name
  - duration (str): Video duration
  - views (str): View count
  - likes (str): Number of likes

Ace Hardware – Product Search

Search for products matching a given query on acehardware.com and extract up to N results.

Parameters:
  - query (str): The search term (e.g., "LED light bulbs")
  - max_results (int): Maximum number of products to extract (e.g., 5)

Returns for each product:
  - product_name (str): Name of the product
  - brand (str): Brand name
  - price (str): Product price
  - product_url (str): URL of the product page

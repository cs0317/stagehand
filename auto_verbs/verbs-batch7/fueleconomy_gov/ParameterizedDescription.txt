Task: Look up fuel economy data for a vehicle on FuelEconomy.gov.

Parameters:
- year (int): Vehicle model year (e.g., 2025)
- make (str): Vehicle make (e.g., "Toyota")
- model (str): Vehicle model (e.g., "Camry")

Extract (all trims):
- trim (str): Full trim name with engine details
- fuel_type (str): Fuel type (Regular Gasoline, Premium, etc.)
- city_mpg (int): City MPG
- highway_mpg (int): Highway MPG
- combined_mpg (int): Combined MPG

Search archive.org for items matching a given query in a specified collection.
Extract up to {max_results} results with:
  - title
  - creator/author
  - date published
  - views/downloads

Parameters:
  search_query: str   (e.g. "history of computing")
  collection: str     (e.g. "texts")
  max_results: int    (e.g. 5)

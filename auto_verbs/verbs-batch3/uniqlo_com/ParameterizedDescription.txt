Uniqlo – Product Search

Parameters:
  - query (str): Search query, e.g. "ultra light down jacket"
  - max_results (int): Maximum number of products to extract, e.g. 5

Steps:
  1. Navigate to https://www.uniqlo.com/us/en/search?q=<query>
  2. Dismiss any modal overlays
  3. Wait for product cards to load
  4. Extract up to max_results products with:
     - product name
     - price
     - available colors (count from color chip elements)
  5. Print the list

ebay_sold_search

Search eBay for completed/sold listings and extract details.
For each sold item: title, sold_price, sold_date, condition.

URL pattern: https://www.ebay.com/sch/i.html?_nkw={query}&LH_Complete=1&LH_Sold=1

Parameters:
  - query: Search term (default: "Nintendo Switch OLED")
  - max_results: Max results to extract (default: 5)

Output: Array of sold item objects with title, sold_price, sold_date, condition

openlibrary_search: Search for books on Open Library.

Parameters:
  - search_query (str): The search term to look for books. Default: "machine learning"
  - max_results (int): Maximum number of book results to return. Default: 5

Returns a list of book items, each containing:
  - title (str): The title of the book
  - author (str): The author(s) of the book
  - first_publish_year (str): The year the book was first published
  - publisher (str): The publisher of the book
  - num_editions (int): The number of editions available
  - isbn (str): The ISBN of the book
  - subjects (str): The subject areas or topics of the book

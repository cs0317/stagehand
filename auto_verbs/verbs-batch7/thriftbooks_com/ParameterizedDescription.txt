ThriftBooks – Book Search

Search ThriftBooks for books by keyword.

Parameters:
- search_query (str): The search query (e.g. "1984 George Orwell"). Default: "1984 George Orwell"
- max_results (int): Maximum number of results to return. Default: 5

Returns:
- books: list of BookResult
  - title (str): Book title
  - author (str): Author name(s)
  - price (str): Price (e.g. "$5.09")
  - condition (str): Condition (e.g. "Good", "New", "Like New")
  - format (str): Format (e.g. "Paperback", "Hardcover")

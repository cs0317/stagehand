newyorker_search: Search for articles on The New Yorker website.

Parameters:
  - search_query (str): The search term to look for articles. Default: "technology"
  - max_results (int): Maximum number of article results to return. Default: 5

Returns a list of article items, each containing:
  - title (str): The title of the article
  - author (str): The author of the article
  - publish_date (str): The publication date of the article
  - section (str): The section/category the article belongs to (e.g., "Culture", "News")
  - summary (str): A brief summary or description of the article

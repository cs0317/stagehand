tipranks_search(ticker: str = "AAPL") -> dict

Search for stock analyst ratings on TipRanks.

Parameters:
  ticker (str): The stock ticker symbol to look up. Default: "AAPL"

Returns:
  dict: A dictionary containing:
    - ticker (str): The stock ticker symbol
    - company_name (str): The company name
    - analyst_consensus (str): The analyst consensus rating (e.g., Strong Buy, Buy, Hold)
    - price_target (str): The average analyst price target
    - smart_score (str): The TipRanks Smart Score
    - num_analysts (str): The number of analysts covering the stock
    - sector (str): The sector the company belongs to

Search for gas prices near a given location on GasBuddy.
Extract up to N gas stations with station name, address, regular gas price, and last updated time.

Parameters:
  location: str — The location to search near (default: "Denver, CO")
  max_results: int — Maximum number of stations to return (default: 5)

Return fields (per station):
  station_name: str — Name of the gas station
  address: str — Street address of the station
  regular_price: str — Price of regular unleaded gas
  last_updated: str — When the price was last updated

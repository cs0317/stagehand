Share an Overleaf project with a collaborator.

Parameters:
- project_id (str): The Overleaf project ID (e.g., "69e6b0a3d05bcdbdf251587c")
- collaborator_email (str): Email address of the collaborator to invite (e.g., "shuochen@live.com")
- role (str): The role to assign — one of "Editor", "Viewer", "Reviewer" (default: "Editor")

Steps:
1. Navigate to https://www.overleaf.com/project/{project_id}
2. Click the "Share" button in the toolbar
3. In the share dialog, select the role from the dropdown (Editor/Viewer/Reviewer)
4. Enter the collaborator's email address
5. Click the invite/share button
6. Return whether the invitation was successful

Verb: bbb_search
Parameters:
  - query: str (default "Comcast") – business name to search
  - max_results: int (default 1) – how many business profiles to return (usually 1 for specific search)

Steps:
  1. Navigate to https://www.bbb.org
  2. Search for the business
  3. Click the first matching result to go to the business profile
  4. Extract BBB rating, accreditation status, customer review rating, and number of complaints
  5. Print the results

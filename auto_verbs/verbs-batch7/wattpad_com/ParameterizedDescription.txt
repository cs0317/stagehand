Wattpad – Story Search

Search Wattpad for stories by keyword.

Parameters:
- search_query (str): The search query (e.g. "fantasy adventure"). Default: "fantasy adventure"
- max_results (int): Maximum number of results to return. Default: 5

Returns:
- stories: list of StoryResult
  - title (str): Story title
  - reads (str): Total reads (e.g. "12.1K", "2.7M")
  - votes (str): Total votes
  - parts (str): Number of parts
  - description (str): Story description (truncated)

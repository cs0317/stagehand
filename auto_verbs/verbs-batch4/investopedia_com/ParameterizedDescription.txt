Search for a financial term on Investopedia and extract its definition and details.
Navigate to the Investopedia search page, click the top result, and extract term details from the article page.

Parameters:
  query: str — The financial term to search for (default: "compound interest")

Return fields:
  term_name: str — The name/title of the financial term
  definition: str — The definition or summary of the term
  key_takeaways: list[str] — Up to 3 key takeaways from the article
  example: str — An example illustrating the term

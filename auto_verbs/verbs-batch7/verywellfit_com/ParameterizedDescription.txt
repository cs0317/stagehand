Verywell Fit – Article Search

Search Verywell Fit for fitness articles by keyword.

Parameters:
- search_query (str): The search query (e.g. "squat exercise guide"). Default: "squat exercise guide"
- max_results (int): Maximum number of results to return. Default: 3

Returns:
- articles: list of ArticleResult
  - title (str): Article title
  - summary (str): Article summary/description

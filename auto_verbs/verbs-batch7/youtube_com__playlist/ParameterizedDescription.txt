Site: youtube.com (playlists)
Task: Extract videos from a YouTube playlist.

URL Pattern:
  https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query={query}
  Then navigate to first playlist result.

Parameters:
  - search_query (str): Search term, default "Lo-Fi Hip Hop Radio"
  - max_results (int): Max videos to extract, default 10

Fields to extract per video:
  - title (string): Video title
  - channel (string): Channel name
  - duration (string): Video duration

Verb: crateandbarrel_search
Parameters:
  - query: str (default "dining table") – product search term
  - max_results: int (default 5) – how many products to return

Steps:
  1. Navigate to https://www.crateandbarrel.com/search?query=dining+table
  2. Wait for results to load
  3. Extract up to max_results products: name, price, dimensions, rating
  4. Print the list

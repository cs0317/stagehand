Athlinks – Search Race Results

Website: https://www.athlinks.com
Task: Search for race results on Athlinks

Parameters:
  - race_name: race name to search (e.g., "Boston Marathon")
  - max_results: maximum number of results to return (default 5)

Fields to extract:
  - runner_name: runner's full name
  - bib_number: bib/race number
  - finish_time: finishing time
  - overall_place: overall placement
  - age_group: age group category
  - age_group_place: placement within age group

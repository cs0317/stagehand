Time and Date – Look Up Current Time

Website: https://www.timeanddate.com
Task: Look up the current time in a city

Parameters:
  - city: city name (e.g., "Tokyo, Japan")

Fields to extract:
  - city_name: city name
  - current_time: current local time
  - date: current date
  - timezone_abbr: timezone abbreviation
  - utc_offset: UTC offset

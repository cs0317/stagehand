Look up a mutual fund on Morningstar by ticker symbol.
Extract fund name, ticker, NAV price, Morningstar rating, expense ratio, and year-to-date return.

Parameters:
  ticker: str — Mutual fund ticker symbol to look up (default: "VFIAX")

Return fields:
  fund_name: str — Full name of the fund
  ticker: str — Ticker symbol
  nav_price: str — Net Asset Value price
  morningstar_rating: str — Morningstar star rating (e.g. "4", "5")
  expense_ratio: str — Fund expense ratio
  ytd_return: str — Year-to-date return percentage

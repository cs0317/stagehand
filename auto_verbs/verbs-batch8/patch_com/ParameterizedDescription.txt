patch_search: Search for local news articles on Patch.

Parameters:
  - search_query (str): The search term to look for local news articles. Default: "community events"
  - max_results (int): Maximum number of article results to return. Default: 5

Returns a list of article items, each containing:
  - title (str): The title of the news article
  - location (str): The local area or neighborhood the article covers
  - publish_date (str): The publication date of the article
  - summary (str): A brief summary or description of the article
  - url (str): The URL link to the full article

Search Reelgood for movies by genre. Extract the top results: title, release year, and detail URL.

Parameters:
- Genre: The genre to filter by (e.g. "thriller")
- num_results: Number of results to extract (default 5)

Steps:
1. Navigate to https://reelgood.com/movies/genre/{genre}
2. Wait for the movie grid to load
3. Skip promoted/ad cards (those containing "Promoted" in text)
4. Extract from each movie card (a[href*="/movie/"]):
   - Title from [data-testid="content-listing-title"]
   - Year from the text pattern "(YYYY)"
   - Detail URL from the href
5. Print the results

Note: IMDb rating, streaming services, and description are only available
on individual movie detail pages, not the genre listing grid.

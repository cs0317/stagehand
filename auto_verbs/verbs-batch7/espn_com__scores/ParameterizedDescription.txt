Task: Extract NBA game scores from ESPN scoreboard.

Parameters:
- max_games (int): Maximum number of games to extract (default: 5)

Extract (per game):
- away_team (str)
- home_team (str)
- away_score (str)
- home_score (str)
- status (str): e.g., "Final", "11:12 - 4th", "5:30 PM"
- series_info (str): e.g., "East 1st Round - Game 1"

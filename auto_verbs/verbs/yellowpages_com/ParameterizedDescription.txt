Website: yellowpages.com
Task: Search for a business type in a given location and extract business listings
Parameters:
  - search_term: string (e.g. "plumber")
  - location: string (e.g. "Chicago, IL")
  - max_results: 5 (number of business listings to extract)
Data to extract per listing:
  - business_name
  - phone_number
  - address
  - rating (e.g. "4.5" or "N/A")

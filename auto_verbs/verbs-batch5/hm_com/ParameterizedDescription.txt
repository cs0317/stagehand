H&M – Clothing Search

Website: https://www2.hm.com
Task: Search for clothing items and extract product info

Parameters:
  - search_query: item to search for (e.g., "winter jacket")
  - max_results: maximum number of products to extract (default: 5)

Fields to extract:
  - product_name: name of the product
  - price: product price
  - color_options: available color options
  - product_url: link to product page

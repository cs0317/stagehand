Search Transfermarkt for a soccer player and extract profile details.

Parameters:
  player_name: str — Name of the player to search for (default: "Kylian Mbappé")

Return fields:
  player_name: str — Full player name
  current_club: str — Current club name
  market_value: str — Estimated market value
  age: str — Player's age
  nationality: str — Player's nationality/citizenship
  position: str — Playing position

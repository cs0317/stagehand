Letterboxd – Film Search

Website: https://letterboxd.com
Task: Search for a film and extract info with popular reviews

Parameters:
  - film_title: film to search for (e.g., "Parasite")

Fields to extract:
  - film_title: title of the film
  - year: release year
  - director: director name
  - average_rating: average rating
  - reviews: list of up to 5 popular reviews with reviewer_name, rating, review_snippet

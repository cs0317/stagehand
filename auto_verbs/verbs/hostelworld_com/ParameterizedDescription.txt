Task: Search Hostelworld

Search for hostels in {city} for {guests} guests on Hostelworld.
Extract up to {max_results} hostels with:
  - hostel name
  - price per night
  - rating
  - distance from city center
Print the list.

Parameters:
  - city: str (default: "Barcelona") — city to search
  - guests: int (default: 2) — number of guests
  - max_results: int (default: 5) — maximum hostels to extract

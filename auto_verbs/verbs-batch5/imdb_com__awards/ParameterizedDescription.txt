IMDb – Academy Awards (Oscars)

Website: https://www.imdb.com
Task: Extract award categories and winners from the most recent Oscar ceremony

Parameters:
  - max_results: maximum number of award categories to extract (default: 5)

Fields to extract:
  - category_name: name of the award category
  - winner_name: name of the winner
  - film_title: title of the film/show

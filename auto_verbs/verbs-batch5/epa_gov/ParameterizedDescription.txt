EPA.gov – Environmental Information Search

Website: https://www.epa.gov
Task: Search for environmental/regulatory info and extract results

Parameters:
  - search_query: text to search for (e.g., "air quality standards")
  - max_results: maximum number of results to extract (default: 5)

Fields to extract:
  - page_title: title of the result page
  - summary: description/snippet of the result
  - url: link to the result page

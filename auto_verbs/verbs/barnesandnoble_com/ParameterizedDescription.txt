Verb: barnesandnoble_search
Parameters:
  - query: str (default "Brandon Sanderson") – search term (author/title)
  - max_results: int (default 5) – how many books to return

Steps:
  1. Navigate to https://www.barnesandnoble.com
  2. Type the query into the search box and submit
  3. Wait for results to load
  4. Extract up to max_results books: title, format, price, rating
  5. Print the list

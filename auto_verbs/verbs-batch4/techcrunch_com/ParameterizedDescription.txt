Search TechCrunch for articles matching a search term
and extract article listing details.

Parameters:
  search_term: str — Topic to search for (default: "artificial intelligence startup")
  max_results: int — Maximum number of articles to return (default: 5)

Return fields (per article):
  headline: str — Article headline
  author: str — Author name
  publication_date: str — Publication date
  summary: str — Category or summary snippet

Task: Browse Green Matters sustainable living articles and extract details.

Parameters:
- topic (str): The topic slug (e.g., "what-is-sustainable-living")
- max_results (int): Maximum number of articles (default: 5)

Extract:
- title (str): Article title
- author (str): Author name
- date (str): Publication date (ISO format)
- summary (str): Article excerpt/summary

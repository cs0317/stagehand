Verb: commonsensemedia_review
Website: https://www.commonsensemedia.org
Task: Look up a game review and extract review details

Parameters:
  - game_slug (str): The game slug in the URL (e.g., "minecraft")
  - max_results (int): Maximum number of results (default: 1, single review page)

Output Fields:
  - title (str): Title of the game
  - age_rating (str): Recommended age (e.g., "age 8+")
  - quality_rating (str): Quality rating in stars (e.g., "4/5")
  - one_liner (str): Short summary of the review
  - parents_need_to_know (str): Summary of what parents need to know

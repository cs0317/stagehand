Numbeo – Cost of Living Comparison

Website: https://www.numbeo.com
Task: Compare cost of living between two cities

Parameters:
  - city1: first city (e.g., "New York, NY")
  - city2: second city (e.g., "London, United Kingdom")

Fields to extract:
  - rent_index: rent index for each city
  - groceries_index: groceries index for each city
  - restaurant_index: restaurant price index for each city
  - overall_index: overall cost of living index for each city

Glassdoor.com – Salary Information Search

Website: https://www.glassdoor.com
Task: Search for salary information for a job title in a location

Parameters:
  - job_title: job title to search for (e.g., "Software Engineer")
  - location: location for salary info (e.g., "San Francisco, CA")

Fields to extract:
  - median_base_salary: median base salary
  - low_estimate: low salary estimate
  - high_estimate: high salary estimate
  - total_compensation: total compensation if available

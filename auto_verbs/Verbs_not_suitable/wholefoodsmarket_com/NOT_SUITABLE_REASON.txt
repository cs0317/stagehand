Whole Foods Market – Product Search: NOT SUITABLE

Reason: The WFM grocery search (wholefoodsmarket.com/grocery/search) is powered
by Amazon Fresh. Displaying product results (names, prices, sizes) requires:

  1. Active Amazon account sign-in (session cookies)
  2. A delivery-eligible ZIP code / address set on the Amazon account

Without both conditions met, the search page shows "delivery isn't available
for your location" and returns zero products. This is a location-gated,
login-gated experience that cannot be reliably automated with a temp Chrome
profile or from a server/codespace environment.

The WFM brand site (/products?q=...) only shows product descriptions without
prices. The Amazon storefront (amazon.com/s?i=wholefoods) triggers bot detection.

Conclusion: Website requires authenticated session + geo-eligible delivery
address — not suitable for a general-purpose automation verb.

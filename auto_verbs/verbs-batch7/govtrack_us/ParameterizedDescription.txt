Task: Search GovTrack for bills on a topic and extract details.

Parameters:
- search_query (str): The search query (e.g., "climate change")
- max_results (int): Maximum number of results (default: 5)

Extract:
- bill_number (str): Display number (e.g., "S. 1471")
- title (str): Bill title
- sponsor (str): Sponsor name
- status (str): Current status label
- introduced_date (str): Date introduced

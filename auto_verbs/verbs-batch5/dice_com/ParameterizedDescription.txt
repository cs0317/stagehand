Verb: dice_search
Website: https://www.dice.com
Task: Search for tech jobs and extract listings

Parameters:
  - search_query (str): Job title/keyword to search for (e.g., "machine learning engineer")
  - max_results (int): Maximum number of results to return (default: 5)

Output Fields:
  - job_title (str): Title of the job listing
  - company_name (str): Name of the company
  - location (str): Job location
  - posted_date (str): When the job was posted
  - salary_range (str): Salary range if available

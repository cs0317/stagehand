Treehugger – Article Search

Search Treehugger for articles by keyword.

Parameters:
- search_query (str): The search query (e.g. "solar panel"). Default: "solar panel"
- max_results (int): Maximum number of results to return. Default: 5

Returns:
- articles: list of ArticleResult
  - title (str): Article title
  - summary (str): Article summary/description

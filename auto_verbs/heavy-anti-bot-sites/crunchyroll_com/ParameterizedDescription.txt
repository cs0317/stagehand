crunchyroll_search

Search Crunchyroll for an anime and extract: title, episodes/seasons, rating, description.

URL pattern: https://www.crunchyroll.com/search?q={query}

Parameters:
  - query: Search term (default: "Attack on Titan")
  - max_results: Max results to extract (default: 5)

Output: Array of anime results with title, episodes, rating, description

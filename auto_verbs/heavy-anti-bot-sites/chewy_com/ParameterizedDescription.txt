Verb: chewy_search
Parameters:
  - query: str (default "dog food grain free") – product search term
  - max_results: int (default 5) – how many products to return

Steps:
  1. Navigate to https://www.chewy.com and search for products
  2. Wait for search results to load
  3. Extract up to max_results products: name, price, rating, brand
  4. Print the list

Angi.com – Search Home Service Professionals

Parameters:
  - service_type (str): The type of service to search for (e.g. "plumber")
  - location (str): The location to search near (e.g. "Phoenix, AZ")
  - max_results (int): Maximum number of professionals to return (e.g. 5)

Steps:
  1. Navigate to https://www.angi.com
  2. Search for professionals matching {service_type} near {location}
  3. Extract up to {max_results} professionals, each with:
     - name
     - rating
     - number of reviews
     - services offered
  4. Return/print the list of professionals

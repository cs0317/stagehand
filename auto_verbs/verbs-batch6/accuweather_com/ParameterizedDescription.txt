AccuWeather – Weather Forecast

Search for weather forecast for a given location on accuweather.com and extract current conditions and 5-day forecast.

Parameters:
  - location (str): The location to search for (e.g., "San Francisco, CA")

Returns:
  - current_temp (str): Current temperature
  - condition (str): Current weather condition (e.g., "Sunny", "Cloudy")
  - high (str): Today's high temperature
  - low (str): Today's low temperature
  - humidity (str): Current humidity percentage
  - wind (str): Current wind speed
  - forecast (list): 5-day forecast with day, high, low, condition

cricbuzz_scores

Extract current/recent live cricket match scores from Cricbuzz live scores page.
For each match, extract: team1, team1_score, team2, team2_score, status, venue, match_info.

URL: https://www.cricbuzz.com/cricket-match/live-scores

Parameters: None (returns current live/recent matches)

Output: Array of match objects with team1, team1_score, team2, team2_score, status, venue, match_info

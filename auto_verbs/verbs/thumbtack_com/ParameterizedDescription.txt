Thumbtack – Service Professional Search

Search for service professionals in a given category and location on thumbtack.com.
Compose a list of up to N results.
Each professional has their name, rating, number of reviews, and starting price.
Print the list.

Parameters:
  service       – type of service to search (e.g. "house cleaning")
  location      – city/state (e.g. "Portland, OR") — used to form the URL slug
  max_results   – maximum number of professionals to return (e.g. 5)

Note: Starting prices are not visible on the listing page; they require
individual profile visits or quote requests. The script reports "N/A" for price.

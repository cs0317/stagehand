Task: Search Grants.gov

Search for grants related to {query} on Grants.gov.
Extract up to {max_results} grants with:
  - title
  - agency
  - funding amount
  - close date
Print the list.

Parameters:
  - query: str (default: "STEM education") — search query
  - max_results: int (default: 5) — maximum number of grants to extract

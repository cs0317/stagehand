TripAdvisor – Hotel Search

Search for hotels in a given destination on tripadvisor.com.
Compose a list of up to N results sorted by Traveler Ranked.
Each hotel has its name, price per night, and traveler rating.
Print the list.

Parameters:
  destination   – city to search (e.g. "San Diego")
  max_results   – maximum number of hotels to return (e.g. 5)

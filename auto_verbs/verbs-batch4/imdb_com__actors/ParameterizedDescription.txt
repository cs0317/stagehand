Search for an actor on IMDB by name, navigate to their profile page,
and extract biographical details along with notable filmography.

Parameters:
  query: str — The actor name to search for (default: "Meryl Streep")
  max_films: int — Maximum number of notable films to return (default: 5)

Return fields:
  actor_name: str — Full name of the actor
  birth_date: str — Date of birth
  biography: str — Short biography text
  films: list — Up to max_films notable films, each with:
    film_title: str — Title of the film
    year: str — Release year
    role: str — Character or role played

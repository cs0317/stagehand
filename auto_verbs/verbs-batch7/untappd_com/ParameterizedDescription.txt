Untappd – Beer Search

Search Untappd for beers by keyword.

Parameters:
- search_query (str): The search query (e.g. "IPA"). Default: "IPA"
- max_results (int): Maximum number of results to return. Default: 5

Returns:
- beers: list of BeerResult
  - name (str): Beer name
  - brewery (str): Brewery name
  - style (str): Beer style
  - abv (str): ABV percentage
  - ibu (str): IBU rating
  - rating (str): User rating (e.g. "3.687")

Task: Search BeerAdvocate for beers of a given style and extract top results.

Parameters:
- beer_style (str): The beer style to search for (e.g., "stout")
- style_id (int): The BeerAdvocate style ID (e.g., 157 for American Imperial Stout)

Extract (top 5):
- beer_name (str)
- brewery (str)
- style (str)
- abv (str)
- rating_score (str)
- num_reviews (str)

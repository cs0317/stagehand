Search for a song on Genius by query, click the top result, and extract song details.
Extract the song title, artist name, album name, and first verse of lyrics from the song page.

Parameters:
  query: str — The search query combining song name and artist (default: "Bohemian Rhapsody Queen")

Return fields:
  song_title: str — Title of the song
  artist: str — Artist / performer name
  album: str — Album name
  first_verse: str — First verse of the song lyrics

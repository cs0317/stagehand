Google Flights – Explore Cheap Destinations

Search for flights from a departure city on Google Flights.
Set travel dates (round-trip, N days).
Use the "Explore" map view to find cheap destinations.
Compose a list of up to N cheap destinations with city name and round-trip price.
Print the list.

Parameters:
  departure_city – city to depart from (e.g. "Chicago")
  trip_days      – number of days for the trip (e.g. 5)
  max_results    – maximum number of destinations to return (e.g. 5)

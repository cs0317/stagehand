thrillist_search(search_query: str = "best bars", max_results: int = 5) -> list[dict]

Search for food and travel articles on Thrillist.

Parameters:
  search_query (str): The search query to look up on Thrillist. Default: "best bars"
  max_results (int): Maximum number of results to return. Default: 5

Returns:
  list[dict]: A list of dictionaries, each containing:
    - title (str): The article title
    - author (str): The article author
    - publish_date (str): The publication date
    - category (str): The article category
    - summary (str): A brief summary of the article

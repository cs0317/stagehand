depop_search

Search Depop for items and extract listing details.
For each listing: item name (from URL slug), brand, price, size, and seller username (from URL slug).

URL pattern: https://www.depop.com/search/?q={query}

Parameters:
  - query: Search term (default: "vintage denim jacket")
  - max_results: Max results to extract (default: 5)

Output: Array of listing objects with item_name, brand, price, size, seller

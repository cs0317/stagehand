Verb: crunchbase_company
Website: https://www.crunchbase.com
Task: Look up a company and extract key details

Parameters:
  - company_slug (str): The company identifier in the URL (e.g., "stripe")

Output Fields:
  - company_name (str): Name of the company
  - description (str): Short description of the company
  - founded_date (str): When the company was founded
  - headquarters (str): Location of headquarters
  - total_funding (str): Total funding amount
  - last_funding_round (str): Details of the last funding round

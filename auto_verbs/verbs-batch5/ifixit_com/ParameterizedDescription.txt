iFixit – Repair Guide Search

Website: https://www.ifixit.com
Task: Search for repair guides and extract guide info

Parameters:
  - search_query: repair guide to search for (e.g., "iPhone 15 battery replacement")
  - max_results: maximum number of guides to extract (default: 3)

Fields to extract:
  - guide_title: title of the repair guide
  - difficulty: difficulty level
  - estimated_time: estimated time to complete
  - num_steps: number of steps
  - guide_url: link to the guide

Slickdeals – Search Deals

Website: https://slickdeals.net
Task: Search for deals on a product

Parameters:
  - query: search term (e.g., "headphones")

Fields to extract:
  - deal_title: deal title
  - price: deal price
  - original_price: original/list price
  - store_name: retailer name
  - thumbs_up: number of thumbs up votes

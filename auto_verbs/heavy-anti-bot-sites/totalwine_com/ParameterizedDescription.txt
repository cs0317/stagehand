TotalWine – Wine Search

Search TotalWine for wines by keyword.

Parameters:
- search_query (str): The search query (e.g. "Pinot Noir"). Default: "Pinot Noir"
- max_results (int): Maximum number of results to return. Default: 5

Returns:
- wines: list of WineResult
  - name (str): Wine name
  - region (str): Wine region
  - price (str): Price
  - rating (str): Customer rating
  - bottle_size (str): Bottle size

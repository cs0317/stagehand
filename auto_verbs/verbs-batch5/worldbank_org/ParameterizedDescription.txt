World Bank – Economic Indicator Lookup

Website: https://data.worldbank.org
Task: Look up economic indicators for a country

Parameters:
  - indicator: indicator name (e.g., "GDP per capita")
  - country: country name (e.g., "Germany")
  - countrySlug: URL slug (e.g., "germany")

Fields to extract:
  - country_name: country name
  - indicator_name: full indicator name (e.g., "GDP per capita (current US$)")
  - most_recent_value: most recent value
  - year: year of most recent value
  - related_indicators: other economic indicators with values and years

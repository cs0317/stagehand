Search HotPads for apartments in a given city under a given monthly price and extract listings.

Parameters:
- city: The city to search (e.g. "Chicago, IL")
- max_price: Maximum monthly rent (e.g. 2000)
- max_results: Number of listings to return (default 5)

Output per listing:
- name: Apartment/building name
- price: Monthly rent or rent range
- bedrooms: Number of bedrooms or range
- location: City, state
- building_type: Type of building (e.g. "Apt building")

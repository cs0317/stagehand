Bankrate.com – Best Savings Rates

Parameters:
  - max_results (int): Maximum number of savings account offers to return (e.g. 5)

Steps:
  1. Navigate to https://www.bankrate.com/banking/savings/best-high-yield-interests-savings-accounts/
  2. Extract up to {max_results} top savings account offers, each with:
     - bank name
     - APY (Annual Percentage Yield)
     - minimum deposit
  3. Return/print the list of offers

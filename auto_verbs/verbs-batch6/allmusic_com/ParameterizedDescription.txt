AllMusic – Artist Info

Search for an artist on allmusic.com and extract artist details and top albums.

Parameters:
  - artist (str): Artist name to search for (e.g., "Miles Davis")
  - max_albums (int): Maximum number of top albums (e.g., 5)

Returns:
  - artist_name (str): Artist name
  - active_years (str): Years active
  - genres (str): Genres
  - bio_summary (str): Biography summary
  - albums (list): Top albums with name, year, rating

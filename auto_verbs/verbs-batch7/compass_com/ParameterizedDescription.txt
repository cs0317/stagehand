Task: Search Compass.com for homes for sale and extract listings.

Parameters:
- location (str): City/neighborhood to search (e.g., "Brooklyn, NY" -> "brooklyn-ny")

Extract (first 5):
- address (str): Street address with unit number
- neighborhood (str): Neighborhood name
- price (str): Listing price
- bedrooms (str): Number of bedrooms
- bathrooms (str): Number of bathrooms
- sqft (str): Square footage (may be "Unavailable")

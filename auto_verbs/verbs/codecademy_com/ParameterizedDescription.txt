Verb: codecademy_search
Parameters:
  - query: str (default "Python") – course search term
  - max_results: int (default 5) – how many courses to return

Steps:
  1. Navigate to https://www.codecademy.com/search?query=Python
  2. Wait for search results to load
  3. Extract up to max_results courses: title, skill level, estimated duration
  4. Print the list

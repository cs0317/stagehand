TripAdvisor – Restaurant Search

Search for restaurants in a given city on tripadvisor.com.
Compose a list of up to N results.
Each restaurant has its name, cuisine type, rating, and price level.
Print the list.

Parameters:
  destination   – city to search (e.g. "New Orleans, LA")
  max_results   – maximum number of restaurants to return (e.g. 5)

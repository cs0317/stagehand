Search Google Maps for public transit directions between two locations.

Parameters:
- origin: Starting location (e.g. "Union Station, Los Angeles")
- destination: Destination (e.g. "Santa Monica Pier")

Output per route:
- duration: Total travel time
- departure_time: Departure time
- arrival_time: Arrival time
- transit_lines: List of transit line names
- fare: Fare if available

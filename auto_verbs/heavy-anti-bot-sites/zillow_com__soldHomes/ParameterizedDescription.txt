Browse recently sold homes on Zillow by location.

Parameters:
- location: str — Location slug for Zillow search (default: "san-francisco-ca")
- max_results: int — Maximum number of sold homes to return (default: 5)

Returns:
- A list of sold homes, each with: address, sold_price, sold_date, beds, baths, sqft, price_per_sqft, listing_type

Trailforks – Mountain Bike Trails

List mountain bike trails in a region from Trailforks.

Parameters:
- region (str): Region slug (e.g. "moab"). Default: "moab"
- max_results (int): Maximum number of trails to return. Default: 5

Returns:
- trails: list of TrailResult
  - name (str): Trail name
  - riding_area (str): Riding area name
  - distance (str): Trail distance (e.g. "6 miles")
  - descent (str): Descent (e.g. "-189 ft")
  - climb (str): Climb (e.g. "821 ft")

Task: Search Goodreads lists and extract top books from the first matching list.

Parameters:
- search_query (str): The search query (e.g., "best science fiction")
- max_results (int): Maximum number of books to extract (default: 5)

Extract:
- list_title (str): Title of the list
- books[]:
  - rank (str): Position on the list
  - title (str): Book title
  - author (str): Author name
  - score (str): List score

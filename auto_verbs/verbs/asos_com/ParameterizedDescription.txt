ASOS.com – Search Products

Parameters:
  - query (str): The search query for products (e.g. "men's jackets")
  - max_results (int): Maximum number of products to return (e.g. 5)

Steps:
  1. Navigate to https://www.asos.com
  2. Search for products matching {query}
  3. Extract up to {max_results} products, each with:
     - product name
     - price
     - brand
  4. Return/print the list of products

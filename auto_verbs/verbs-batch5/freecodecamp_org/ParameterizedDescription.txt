freeCodeCamp.org – Tutorial Search

Website: https://www.freecodecamp.org
Task: Search for tutorials and extract article info

Parameters:
  - search_query: text to search for (e.g., "Python web scraping")
  - max_results: maximum number of articles to extract (default: 5)

Fields to extract:
  - title: article title
  - author: article author
  - publication_date: date published
  - tags: article tags/topics

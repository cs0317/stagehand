minimalistbaker_search - Search for plant-based recipes on Minimalist Baker

Parameters:
  - search_query (str): The recipe or ingredient to search for. Default: "vegan pasta"
  - max_results (int): Maximum number of results to return. Default: 5

Returns a list of recipe items, each containing:
  - recipe_name (str): Name of the recipe
  - total_time (str): Total preparation and cook time (e.g., "30 minutes")
  - servings (str): Number of servings the recipe makes
  - rating (str): Recipe rating from users
  - description (str): Brief description of the recipe
  - diet_tags (str): Dietary tags (e.g., "vegan, gluten-free, oil-free")

IMDb – TV Show Info

Search for a TV show on imdb.com.
Navigate to the TV show page.
Extract the show rating and number of seasons.
Compose a list of up to N top rated episodes.
Each episode has its title, season/episode number, and rating.
Print the list.

Parameters:
  show_name     – TV show to search for (e.g. "Breaking Bad")
  max_results   – maximum number of episodes to return (e.g. 5)

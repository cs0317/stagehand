Get driving directions on MapQuest from one location to another.
Extract total distance, estimated travel time, and step-by-step driving directions.

Parameters:
  from_location: str — Starting address or place (default: "Times Square, New York, NY")
  to_location: str — Destination address or place (default: "Central Park, New York, NY")

Return fields:
  from_location: str — Starting location used
  to_location: str — Destination location used
  total_distance: str — Total driving distance
  estimated_time: str — Estimated travel time
  steps: list[DirectionStep] — Step-by-step driving directions
    instruction: str — Turn-by-turn instruction text
    distance: str — Distance for this step

Verb: dev_to_search
Website: https://dev.to
Task: Search for articles and extract results

Parameters:
  - search_query (str): The topic to search for (e.g., "React hooks")
  - max_results (int): Maximum number of results to return (default: 5)

Output Fields:
  - title (str): Article title
  - author (str): Author name
  - publication_date (str): When the article was published
  - reactions (str): Number of reactions
  - comments (str): Number of comments

Search for an encyclopedia article on britannica.com and extract the article title, introductory summary paragraph, and key facts.

Parameters:
  - search_term (str): The topic to search for on Britannica. Default: "photosynthesis"
  - max_facts (int): Maximum number of key facts to extract. Default: 10

Returns:
  - title (str): The article title
  - summary (str): The introductory summary paragraph
  - facts (list[str]): Key facts or data points from the article
  - url (str): The URL of the article

Search RogerEbert.com for a movie review. Extract the review details.

Parameters:
- movie_slug: URL slug for the movie review (e.g. "parasite-movie-review-2019")

Steps:
1. Navigate to https://www.rogerebert.com/reviews/{movie_slug}
2. Wait for content to load
3. Extract:
   - Title from h1
   - Star rating from .star-box .filled class "starNN" (NN / 10 = rating out of 4)
   - Review date from text matching "Month DD, YYYY" pattern
   - Author from a[href*="/contributors/"] link
   - First paragraph (first <p> with >100 chars in the review body)
4. Print results

Notes:
- Star rating is encoded in CSS class like "star40" = 4.0, "star35" = 3.5, etc.
- The star images are SVG overlays (filled vs unfilled)
- Go up 8 parent levels from .star-box to find the review section container

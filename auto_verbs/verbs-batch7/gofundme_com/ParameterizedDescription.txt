Task: Search GoFundMe campaigns and extract details.

Parameters:
- search_query (str): The search query (e.g., "education")
- max_results (int): Maximum number of results (default: 5)

Extract:
- title (str): Campaign title
- organizer (str): Organizer name
- raised (str): Amount raised so far
- progress_pct (str): Percentage of goal reached
- url (str): Campaign URL

Verb: carvana_search
Parameters:
  - query: str (default "Honda Civic") – car search term
  - max_results: int (default 5) – how many cars to return

Steps:
  1. Navigate to https://www.carvana.com and search for cars
  2. Wait for search results to load
  3. Extract up to max_results cars: year, trim, price, mileage, monthly payment
  4. Print the list

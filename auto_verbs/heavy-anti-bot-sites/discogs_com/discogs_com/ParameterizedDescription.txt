discogs_search

Search Discogs for releases and extract: title, format, label, year, lowest price.

URL pattern: https://www.discogs.com/search/?q={query}&type=release

Parameters:
  - query: Search term (default: "Pink Floyd Dark Side of the Moon")
  - max_results: Max results to extract (default: 5)

Output: Array of release objects with title, format, label, year, lowest_price

Amazon – Product Reviews

Search for a product on amazon.com by query.
Click on the first search result to navigate to the product page.
Navigate to the customer reviews section.
Compose a list of up to N reviews.
Each review has its star rating, title, and review text.
Print the list.

Parameters:
  query         – search query string (e.g. "wireless earbuds")
  max_results   – maximum number of reviews to return (e.g. 5)

Awwwards – Browse Award-Winning Websites

Website: https://www.awwwards.com
Task: Browse award-winning websites on Awwwards

Parameters:
  - max_results: maximum number of sites to return (default 5)

Fields to extract:
  - site_name: website/project name
  - agency: agency or creator name
  - country: agency country
  - award_type: type of award (Site of the Day, Honorable Mention, etc.)
  - score: overall score
  - site_url: URL of the awarded website

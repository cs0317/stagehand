Whole Foods Market – Product Search

Parameters:
  - query (str): Product search query (e.g. "organic coffee")
  - max_results (int): Maximum number of products to return (e.g. 5)

Steps:
  1. Navigate to https://www.wholefoodsmarket.com
  2. Search for products matching {query}
  3. Extract up to {max_results} results, each with:
     - product name
     - price
     - size / weight
  4. Return/print the list of results

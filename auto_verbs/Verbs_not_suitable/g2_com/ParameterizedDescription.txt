G2.com – Software Review Search

Website: https://www.g2.com
Task: Search for software reviews and extract product/review info

Parameters:
  - product_name: software product to search for (e.g., "Slack")
  - max_reviews: maximum number of review summaries to extract (default: 5)

Fields to extract:
  - product_name: name of the software product
  - overall_rating: overall star rating
  - num_reviews: total number of reviews
  - reviews: list of review summaries:
    - reviewer_title: reviewer's job title
    - rating: individual review rating
    - pros: pro comments
    - cons: con comments

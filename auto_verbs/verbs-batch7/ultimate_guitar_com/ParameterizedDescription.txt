Ultimate Guitar – Tab Search

Search Ultimate Guitar for guitar tabs and chords.

Parameters:
- search_query (str): The search query (e.g. "Wonderwall Oasis"). Default: "Wonderwall Oasis"
- max_results (int): Maximum number of results to return. Default: 5

Returns:
- tabs: list of TabResult
  - title (str): Song title/version
  - artist (str): Artist name
  - rating (str): Rating count
  - type (str): Type (Chords, Tab, Guitar Pro, etc.)

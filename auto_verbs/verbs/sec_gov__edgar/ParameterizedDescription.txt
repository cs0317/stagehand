Website: sec.gov/cgi-bin/browse-edgar
Task: Search for SEC filings and extract filing listings
Parameters:
  - company_name: string (e.g. "Tesla")
  - ticker: string (e.g. "TSLA")
  - max_results: 5 (number of filings to extract)
Data to extract per filing:
  - filing_type (e.g. "10-K", "10-Q")
  - filing_date
  - description
  - filing_link (URL to filing document)

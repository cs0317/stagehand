Look up demographic and economic data for a US location on DataUSA.

Parameters:
- search_query: str — The US city, state, or location to search for (default: "Chicago")
- max_results: int — Maximum number of results to return (default: 5)

Returns:
- A list of location results, each with: location_name, population, median_income, poverty_rate, median_age, employment_rate

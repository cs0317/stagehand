PayScale – Salary Data

Website: https://www.payscale.com
Task: Search for salary data for a job title

Parameters:
  - job_title: job title (e.g., "Data Scientist")

Fields to extract:
  - average_salary: average salary
  - salary_range_low: low end of salary range
  - salary_range_high: high end of salary range
  - top_skills: top-paying skills if shown

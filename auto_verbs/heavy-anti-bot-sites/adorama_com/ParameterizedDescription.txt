Task: Search Adorama for a given search query, extract the top N results with product details, and print them.

Parameters:
- search_query (str): The product search term (e.g., "mirrorless camera")
- max_results (int): Maximum number of results to extract (e.g., 5)

For each result, extract:
- product_name (str)
- brand (str)
- price (str)
- customer_rating (str)
- availability (str)

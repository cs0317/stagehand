Search IsThereAnyDeal for a game and extract pricing info from the detail page.

Parameters:
- query: Game to search for (e.g. "Baldur's Gate 3")

Output:
- game_name: Game title
- current_best_price: Current best price
- current_best_store: Store with current best price
- all_time_low: Historical lowest price
- all_time_low_store: Store that had historical low
- regular_price: Regular/full price
- store_prices: List of stores with their current and historical low prices

Search for places and venues on Foursquare by location and keyword.

Parameters:
- location: str — The city or area to search near (default: "New York")
- search_query: str — The type of venue to search for (default: "coffee")
- max_results: int — Maximum number of venues to return (default: 5)

Returns:
- A list of venues, each with: venue_name, category, address, rating, price_tier, num_tips
